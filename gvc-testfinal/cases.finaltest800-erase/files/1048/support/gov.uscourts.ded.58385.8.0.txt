ZONOFF INC.'S MOTION TO DISMISS
AND FOR A MORE DEFINITE STATEMENT

Pursuant to Rule 12(b)(6) and 12(e) of the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure, and for the
reasons set forth in the accompanying memorandum in support hereof, defendant Zonoff Inc.
("Zonoff') respectfully moves the Court to dismiss plaintiff Icontrol Networks, Inc.'s
("Icontrol") claims for willful infringement, to dismiss Icontrol's claim for pre-suit damages, and
to require a more definite statement of the Complaint identifying the specific claims of the three

patents-in-suit that are alleged to be indirectly infringed by Zonoff.
