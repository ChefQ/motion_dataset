PLAINTIFF'S UNOPPOSED MOTION TO SEAL EXHIBITS PURSUANT TO
PROTECTIVE ORDER AND LOCAL RULE 1.09
Pursuant to M.D. L.R. 1.09(b) and the Court's Protective Order of March 26, 2007 [Dkt.
27], Plaintiff John J. Thornton ("Thornton") hereby moves to seal Defendants' Trial Exhibit Nos.
334, 349, 355, 356.

These document have been designated as "Attorneys Eyes Only" by

Defendants under the Protective Order. The exhibits are various licensing agreements between
Defendants and third parties. Mr. Thornton is filing these exhibits so that they can be considered
in connection with his concurrently filed Motion to Strike Documents From Defendants' Exhibit
List and for the Imposition of Sanctions Under Fed. R. Civ. P. 37(B) and Memorandum of Law
in Support. Pursuant to L.R. 1.09(b)(iii) and the Protective Order, Thornton respectfully requests
that the identified documents remain under seal for the duration of this litigation or until the
documents are otherwise introduced into evidence at trial.
WHEREFORE, Mr. Thornton respectfully requests entry of an Order directing the
Clerk to accept and seal Defendants' Trial Exhibits 334, 349, 355, 356 in connection with his
concurrently filed Motion to Strike Documents From Defendants' Exhibit List and for the

Imposition of Sanctions Under Fed. R. Civ. P. 37(B) and Memorandum of Law in Support.

Plaintiff's counsel conferred with counsel for Defendants regarding the relief requested
herein. Defendants do not oppose this Motion to Seal.
