MOTION TO REMAND
COMES NOW the Plaintiff City of Birmingham Retirement & Relief System, individually
and on behalf of all others similarly situated, and move this Court pursuant to 28 U.S.C. §1447(c) for
an Order remanding this action to the Circuit Court for Jefferson County, Alabama, where it was
properly filed in the first instance and as grounds therefore states as follows:
Plaintiff’s motion to remand should be granted because Defendants improperly removed this
action in violation of 15 U.S.C. §77v(a), which provides: “Except as provided in section 77p(c) of
this title, no case arising under [the Securities Act of 1933] and brought in any State court of
competent jurisdiction shall be removed to any court of the United States.” Plaintiff has asserted
