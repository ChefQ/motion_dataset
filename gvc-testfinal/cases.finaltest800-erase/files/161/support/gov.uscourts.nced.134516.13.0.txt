DEFENDANT BANKUNITED, N.A.’S
MOTION TO DISMISS

Now Comes Defendant BankUnited, NA (BankUnited), by and through its undersigned
counsel, and moves the Court to dismiss the claims alleged in the Complaint pursuant to Rule
12(b)(6) of the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure for Failure to State a Claim upon which relief
can be granted. Specifically, Plaintiff’s claim under the Fair Credit Reporting Act fails to allege
facts sufficient to survive this motion under Bell Atlantic Corp. v. Twombly, 550 U.S. 544, 127
S.Ct. 1955 (2007) and Ascroft v. Iqbal, 556 U.S. 662, 127 S. Ct. 1937 (2009).
