On March 14, 2005, Defendant’s Motion was filed and served on the undersigned

counsel of record.
2.

Du ring the week of March 14, 2005, the undersigned counsel of record was ill with

the flu and as a result of this illness was out of the office most of the week of March 21, 2005.
3.

An extension of the time within which EEOC may respond to Defendant’s Motion is

necessary to allow the undersigned counsel of record time to recover from her illness so that she can
complete EEOC’s response to Defendant’s Motion.
