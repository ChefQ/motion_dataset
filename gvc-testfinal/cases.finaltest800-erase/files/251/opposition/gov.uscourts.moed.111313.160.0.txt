AMEREN MISSOURI’S RESPONSE TO UNITED STATES’ MOTION FOR
VOLUNTARY DISMISSAL OF CERTAIN CLAIMS IN THE AMENDED COMPLAINT
Ameren does not oppose the United States’ motion to voluntarily dismiss the claims set
forth in its Motion, ECF No. 157.
However, in accord with its statements on the record at the status conference held on
October 1, 2013, Ameren submits this response to make clear that it is reserving its rights to seek

States now seeks dismissal.
